Our Story

The journey to where we are today has been an incredible experience. We have been lucky enough to be blessed with three beautiful healthy daughters Jessica, Sophie and Olivia.
As many of you already know or anyone with children will tell you it can be very rewarding and full of amazing times but also it can be stressful and not all plain sailing!

However we would not change any of this for the world and we now feel the time is right to be joined in marriage and take that final step towards completing our family.

  • October 2007

    Love at first sight!

    Louise and I met in 2007 by chance when we were both on seperate holidays with friends in Southern Spain. Here is the first photo of us together!

  • December 2008

    Jessica born!

    After we first met we kept in touch even though we lived in different parts of the country. In 2008 this led to a whilrwind romance and Jessica came along at the end of that year!

  • 2009

    San Francisco

    In 2009 with Jessica we spent a brief period living and working in San Franciso where my Dad lives. We had our own apartment in the Marina district of the city and I worked for a design agency, a small almost unknown company called Twitter were located next door! We had planned to remain in California however due to unforseen visa issues we had to return to the UK.

  • 2010/2011

    Back in the UK and moving to London

    With the unexpected return to the UK we had to restablish ourselves in England during difficult times, we briefly lived in Portsmouth and then Coventry.
    In late 2010, an opportunity arose to work in London and after several months commuting that lead to us moving to Kingston upon Thames area in Greater London permanently.

  • June 2012

    Sophie born!

    In London we finally found our feet and we over the moon to welcome our second daughter to the world in the summer of 2012.

  • December 2014

    Justin Proposes!

    With a third baby on the way and being more settled as a family, I thought the time was right to ask Louise if she would be my wife! So we went to Paris for Louise's birthday where I proposed at the top of the Eiffel Tower.

  • February 2015

    Olivia born

    We like surpises and as with both Jessica and Sophie we did not find out the sex of the baby. On February 24th 2015 Louise gave birth to our third daughter Olivia, the day of my Granddad and Olivia's Great Grandad's 96th birthday!

  • We get married
    27th August 2016!
